```mermaid
  service mcn(f5xc:multi-cloud-network-connect)[Network Connect] in xc

  service dns(f5xc:dns-management)[DNS Management] in xc
  service dapp(f5xc:distributed-apps)[Distributed Apps] in xc

  mcn:R --> L:mca
  mca:R --> L:dns
  dns:R --> L:dapp

  mca:B --> T:vm
```
